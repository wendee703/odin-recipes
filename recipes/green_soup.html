Green Veggie Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 Zucchini
  • 3 Carrots
  • 2 Celery Stalks
  • 1 Carton of Bone broth
  • 1 Potato
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chicken Boullion
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Chop up veggies.
  2. Sautee with oil until fragrant, add italian seasoning, chicken boullion and other seasonings to taste.
  3. Add Bone Broth and water until veggies are fully submerged.
  4. Leave on medium heat until veggies are soft.
  5. Use blender or Immersion blender creating a smooth soup.
  6. Adjust flavor to taste.
  7. Serve and top with parmesean cheese and side of toast.
