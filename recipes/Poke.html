Ahi Tuna Poke

Ingredients

  • 2 Ahi Tuna Filets
  • 1/2 Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp Peanut Oil
  • 3 tbsp Mirin
  • 3 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Ginger
  • 2 tbps Garlic
  • Seasame Seeds
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Dice ahi tuna into cubes.
  2. Finely chop ginger and garlic.
  3. Mix soy sauce, peanut oil, mirin, rice vinegar and sugar to taste.
  4. Combine ahi and soy sauce mixture.
  5. Place in refrigerator for ahi to marinate
  6. Serve with a salad or over white Rice.
