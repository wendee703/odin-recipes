Lomo Saltado

Ingredients

  • 3lbs Tritip
  • 4 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 2tbsp Ginger
  • 2tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Cup Red Wine Vinegar
  • Cilantro
  • Oil of choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • French Fries
  • White Rice

Steps

  1. Cut tritip into small strips. Season with salt, pepper, and a dash of cumin. Set aside. Do not add too much salt since you will be adding soy sauce.*
  2. Chop onion and tomatoes Julienne style, removing seeds from tomatoes.
  3. Mine Garlic and Ginger as finely as possible.
  4. Mix soy sauce and red wine vinegar
  5. In a wok, add a bit of oil and add the tritip with some space to allow to char. Do not cramp to avoid meat releasing water. Set aside once tritip is cooked.
  6. In the same wok/pan, add oil. On high heat, add the red onion, allow to cook for 2min. Add garlic and ginger, sautee for 2min without burning.
  7. Add tritip, soy sauce mixture, and cilantro. Allow flavors to combine for 2-3min then add tomatoes at the end so that they do not overcook.
  8. Serve Lomo Saltado over french fries with a side of white rice.
